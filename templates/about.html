Our Mission

ShelfSpace was created with a simple mission: to provide book lovers with an all-in-one platform to browse, discover, shop, and review books. ShelfSpace aims to provide you with a platform that combines the best parts of a bookstore and a review site. With reliable moderation, you can be sure that the reviews you're reading are created by other readers just like you. We at ShelfSpace are always open to criticism and suggestions, and urge you to leave us feedback in the feedback form under the contact page. Thanks for stopping by!

- ShelfSpace Team
